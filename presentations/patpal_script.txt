Petpal!

PetPal is a mobile and web application for a chain of veterinary clinics across the Scottish Highlands. The application allows pet owners to book appointments, view their pet's vaccination and treatment history, receive automated reminders, and pay invoices online. Vets can access patient records, add treatment notes, and generate prescriptions.

Given staff and budget constraints, the methodology for this project will be Agile, within the scrum framework. Each sprint will last 2 weeks, and the product owner here will be the clinic's practice manager.


[SECTION 1]
[NEXT SLIDE]
The first 6 weeks will be spent developing a RESTful API that both the application and the web service will be communicating with. This is because in order to develop the app as well as the web service, we first need to establish the platform; this will ensure reliable behaviour across the two mediums.

Next, we will assign 6 weeks to the development of the app as well as the web service,

The remaining 4 weeks will be spent debugging, optimizing, and ensuring that the application works flawlessly on low-end hardware and in areas with poor and intermittent internet connection.
